About PowerCoders

PowerCoders is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping refugees and migrants build a career in IT through coding training and mentorship.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity, empowering individuals through technology and providing access to the digital world.

About Lausanne

Lausanne is a vibrant city in the French-speaking part of Switzerland. Known for its cultural diversity, it's home to numerous institutions and a hub for international organizations.

Venue: Espace Dickens

We are gathering at Espace Dickens, located at Av. Charles Dickens 4, 1006 Lausanne. This cultural space offers an ideal environment for collaboration and learning.
